About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to creating elite professionals for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. Our focus is strictly on preparing individuals to excel in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We bridge the gap between theory and execution by emphasizing the link between cutting-edge academic insight—through our esteemed UK university partnerships—and hands-on, real-world industry projects.
